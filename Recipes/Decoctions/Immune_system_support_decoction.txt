====== Immune system support decoction ======

This immune system support decoction works to both stimulate the lymphatic system to encourage the production of more white blood cells, and target bacteria or viruses, as well.

====== Ingredients ======

* Water (3 cups)
* Ginger (2 tablespoons, roughly chopped)
* Garlic (1 tablespoon, finely chopped)
* Echinacea (1 tablespoon)
* Spilanthes (1 teaspoon)
* Nettle (2 tablespoons)
* Hyssop (1 tablespoon)
* Honey (2 tablespoons)

====== Time ======

* Preparation: ~5 minutes
* Cooking time: ~35 minutes.

====== Yield ======

Approximately 2 cups.

====== Instructions ======

1. Pour water, ginger, garlic, echinacea, spilanthes, nettle, and hyssop (all ingredients except honey) to a small pot.
2. Raise water to boil, then cover, and simmer over low to medium heat.
3. Let simmer for 30 minutes.
4. Turn off heat and let sit for 5 minutes.
5. Strain the decoction.
6. Serve in a mug, letting decoction cool to room temperature, then add honey to taste.
