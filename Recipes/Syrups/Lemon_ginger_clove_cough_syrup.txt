====== Lemon ginger clove cough syrup ======

This is a description for the "lemon ginger clove cough syrup" recipe.

====== Ingredients ======

* Cloves (1/4 cup)
* Lemons (2, sliced)
* Ginger (1 cup, crushed)
* Honey (1/4 cup)
* Water (3 cups)

====== Time ======

* Preparation: ~15 minutes
* Cooking time: ~1 hour

====== Yield ======

Fills 4 to 5 2oz bottles.

====== Instructions ======

1. In a small saucepan, add all ingredients, except for honey.
2. Bring the mixture to a boil, then cover.
3. Bring down to slightly below a boil, leaving covered, but stirring every 15-20 minutes.
4. Turn the heat off.
5. Strain the herbs from the liquid, pouring the liquid into a bowl to keep.
6. Dispose (compost if possible!) the used herbs.
7. Let the liquid sit uncovered, letting cool for 15 minutes minimum.
8. Add the honey to the mixture.
9. Bottle the syrup!

====== Storage ======

Properly made lemon ginger clove cough syrup using honey as the preservative, as in this recipe, should last about 2 to 3 months. It should be stored in a dry, dark area at room temperature.
